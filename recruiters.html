Your sourcing sheet is the dialer

Most desks live in spreadsheets. Import candidate lists straight from Google Sheets or CSV — names, numbers, emails, and notes auto-map. Keep separate lists per req, per client, or per campaign, and pin the one you're working today.

Notes that survive back-to-back screens

After ten screens, calls blur together. Drop a note on each candidate as you hang up, tag them Hot, Warm, or Cold, and see when you last spoke. Next time the req reopens, your shortlist is already built.

Book the screen before you hang up

A candidate says "Thursday afternoon works" — create the iOS calendar event right from the app. No answer? Fire a one-tap text, WhatsApp message, or email from a template instead of typing the same follow-up forty times a day.

Pace your call block

Set a countdown between calls to leave time for ATS updates, or run Rocket mode through a callback list. Calls come from your own number, so candidates see a real person — and callbacks land on the phone that's actually in your hand.

Candidate data stays on your phone

Everything is stored on-device. No cloud account, no server, no candidate data sitting in a third-party dialer's database — a clean answer when clients ask how you handle personal data.
