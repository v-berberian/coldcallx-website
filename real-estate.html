Built for skip-traced lists

Every owner number, one lead

Skip traces often return three or four numbers per owner. Cold Call X keeps multi-number leads together, so you work the property — not a pile of duplicate rows. Tag motivated sellers Hot, note the asking price, and see at a glance when you last called.

Dial like a wholesaler

Driving for dollars paid off — now get through the list. Rocket mode dials the next owner the moment you hang up, or set a countdown to leave yourself prep time between calls. Every call goes out from your own number through the iPhone dialer: no VoIP line, no per-minute fees.

Real estate dialing, answered

Does Cold Call X work with skip-traced lists?+

Yes. Any skip-tracing service that exports CSV works — PropStream, BatchLeads, DealMachine, REISift, and others. Import the CSV or a Google Sheet and columns like owner name, phone numbers, and notes auto-map.

Can one seller have multiple phone numbers?+

Yes. Multi-number leads are built in, so an owner with three skip-traced numbers stays one lead instead of three duplicate rows.

Do I need a VoIP number for real estate cold calling?+

No. Cold Call X dials through your iPhone's native phone app from your own number. There is no VoIP line to rent and no per-minute fees, and your caller ID stays consistent.
