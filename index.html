FAQ — cold calling on iPhone

Questions, answered straight.

Is Cold Call X an auto dialer for iPhone?+

Yes. Cold Call X is an auto dialer built for iOS. It dials through your call list using your iPhone's native phone app — choose manual one-tap dialing, timed auto-call delays, or 0-second Rocket mode.

How do I import my call list?+

Import leads from any CSV file or directly from Google Sheets. Columns like name, phone number, email, and notes are auto-mapped, and large lists are supported.

Does Cold Call X require a subscription?+

No. Cold Call X is a one-time purchase of $12.99 on the App Store. There are no subscriptions, seats, or hidden fees, and no account is required.

Is my lead data private?+

Completely. All leads, notes, tags, and call activity stay on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers, there is no tracking, and no data is linked to your identity.

Can I send follow-up messages from the app?+

Yes — send texts, WhatsApp messages, or emails with customizable templates, and schedule callback reminders as iOS calendar events.

What do I need to run Cold Call X?+

An iPhone running iOS 17.6 or later. Download Cold Call X from the App Store and import your first list in under a minute.
