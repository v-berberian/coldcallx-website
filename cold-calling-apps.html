What to look for in a cold calling app

• Fast list import. You should be able to load a CSV or Google Sheet with auto-mapped columns and start dialing in under a minute.

• Auto-dialing. The whole point of a cold calling app is to move through a list without retyping numbers — look for hands-free or one-tap dialing with adjustable speed.

• A lightweight CRM. Tags, notes, last-called dates, and multi-number leads keep a big list workable without a separate tool.

• Smart filters. Timezone and called/uncalled filters mean you always dial the right lead at the right time.

• Follow-ups. Calendar callbacks and one-tap text, WhatsApp, or email templates turn a conversation into a booked next step.

• Honest pricing. Decide whether you need cloud VoIP features billed monthly, or prefer to dial from your own number at a one-time cost.
